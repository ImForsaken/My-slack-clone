Welcome to our slack clone app!

Feel free to join conversations in the channels section or contact other users directly via direct message.

To join a channel, click on the channels button and then on the 'open channels' tab. A dialog opens where you can select and add the desired channel. The added channel will then appear on the left side in the menu. Select a chat by clicking on it and the chat history is displayed.

The same applies to interacting with another user directly via direct messages.

If you have further questions, simply click on the info icon in the header.
Now have fun and enjoy using our app.
